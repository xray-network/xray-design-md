PROMPT / MINI APP

Start with the system already decided.

Copy this brief when generating or building an XRAY mini app. It carries the essential layout, behavior, and product constraints with it.

XRAY INTERFACE BRIEF

Create a responsive XRAY/Network mini app for multichain portfolio activity and asset transfers. Use https://design.xraynetwork.io/DESIGN.md as the single source of truth for colors, typography, components, themes, spacing, and interaction states. Use https://design.xraynetwork.io/index.html as a visual reference.

  • Layout: centered content with a max-width of 1240px and responsive side gutters.
  • Purpose: keep each mini app focused on one clear job and one primary action.
  • Responsive: support phone, tablet, and desktop without horizontal overflow.
  • Themes: provide complete light and dark modes using semantic tokens.
  • Context: always show the active wallet, account, network, and selected asset where relevant.
  • States: include loading, empty, error, success, disabled, hover, focus, and pressed states.
  • Data: use mono type for addresses, hashes, tickers, fees, and machine-readable values.
  • Access: ensure keyboard navigation, visible focus, clear labels, and touch targets of at least 40px.

06 — Principles

Restraint makes the system recognizable.

Every choice reinforces legibility, trust, and consistency across chains.

Do

  • Use blue for actions and selected states.
  • Use mono for hashes, addresses, and tickers.
  • Let hairlines create depth in both themes.
  • Keep financial content dense and scannable.

Do not

  • Use gradients, glows, or decorative blue.
  • Introduce photography or illustration.
  • Hide critical fees or transaction context.
  • Use shadows in any theme or component.
